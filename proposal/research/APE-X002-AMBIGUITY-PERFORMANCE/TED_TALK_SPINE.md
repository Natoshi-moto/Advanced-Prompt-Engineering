# Talk spine — The Agent Acts Before the Sentence Settles

**State:** `CANDIDATE`  
**Purpose:** A talk structure, not a claim of TED acceptance or validated
research.

## One-sentence thesis

Chatbots could leave ambiguity on the screen; agents must collapse language
into action, and the dangerous step is allowing the agent's chosen meaning to
inherit the user's authority.

## Opening: the correction

I told an AI that every letter I type has downstream consequences. It answered
as if I had claimed precise control over every letter. The response was useful,
intelligent, rigorous—and not what I had said.

That is harmless while the model is writing prose. What happens when it can
send, trade, delete, publish, invite, diagnose, or deploy?

## Movement 1 — A sentence can hold several actions

Use one compact example: "Kill the position." Do not linger on sensational
examples. Show lexical meaning, target identity, quantity, timing, authority,
and success criteria as separate unresolved variables.

## Movement 2 — Our vocabulary already warns us

An actor was a doer and driver before the word settled on the stage. A
performance was a completion before it was a show. A theater was a place for
viewing before it became a theater of war. A display unfolds something for
view; it is not the whole reality.

Mark this explicitly as a mnemonic lens, not etymological proof.

## Movement 3 — The missing boundary

```text
what was said
  != what the model inferred
  != what the user authorized
  != what the tool executed
  != what actually happened
```

Current interfaces often compress all five into one flowing conversation.

## Movement 4 — The antivirus

Demonstrate an action contract: stable recipient, exact scope, cost, authority,
reversibility, expected effect, and a confirmation bound to those resolved
bytes. The model can propose. The firewall compiles and gates. The tool accepts
only the authorized contract. The receipt checks the world.

## Movement 5 — Sovereignty at low power

The end state is not another social network that owns the graph. It is a local
social computer that can speak across many transports. IRC, signed relay
events, offline bundles, mesh links, and slow connections become adapters. A
small local model helps a person navigate their own corpus. No single platform
gets to be the memory, identity, audience, or economic gatekeeper.

## Ending

We do not make agents safe by demanding that language stop being ambiguous.
We make them safer by refusing to let an interpretation masquerade as
authority.

The pause is not friction added to intelligence. At the moment of action, the
pause is part of intelligence.
